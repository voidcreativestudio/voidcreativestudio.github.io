SOLITUDE

A commission by SONY Music. An Audio / Visual installation to raise awareness over Mental Health Awareness Week. Representing the mental wellbeing at SONY MUSIC.

Details

Employees answered the anonymous question “How is your mental health today?”. Based on the cumulative average over the week, the suspended cloud surged with colour to update its global RGB value. A piece of classical music helped form an audio soundscape that became more distorted with lower results and clearer with higher results. The installation also served as a relaxation and contemplation space.

Technologies

Software written in OpenFrameworks (C++) deplyed on a Raspberry Pi 3b+.

Input : Bluetooth keyboard.

Output : Audio soundscape, Video and custom LED lighting.
