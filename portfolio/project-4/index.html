TOGETHER

The installation requires and encourages participants to work together. We can only solve global environmental issues together.

Details

Touching conductive silhouettes causes virtual plastic waste to be lifted from the ocean. As multiple interact with different silhouettes, the plastic is removed at a greater rate.

The installation hopes to inspire people to work together to overcome common environmental concerns.
